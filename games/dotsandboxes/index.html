How to Play Dots & Boxes

  • Players alternate turns joining two horizontally or vertically adjacent dots with a line.
  • A player who completes the fourth side of a 1x1 Box earns one point and takes another turn!
  • The game ends when no more lines can be placed. The player with the most boxes wins!
