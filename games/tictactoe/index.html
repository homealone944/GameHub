How to Play

A classic game for two players!

  • Take turns placing your mark ('X' or 'O') in an empty square.
  • The first player to align 3 marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.
  • If all 9 squares are filled and no player has 3 in a row, it's a Draw.
