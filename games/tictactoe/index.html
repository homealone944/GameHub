How to Play Tic-Tac-Toe

A classic game for two players!

  • Players take turns placing their mark ('X' or 'O') in an empty square on the 3x3 grid.
  • The first player to align 3 of their marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins the game.
  • If all 9 squares are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game ends in a Draw.
