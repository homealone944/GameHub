How to Play Codenames Duet

You and your partner are on a secret mission. You must correctly identify all 15 green agent words on the board before you run out of turns!


The Catch: Each player is only looking at their specific half of the Key Card via the "INTEL" phase. Your half shows you 9 green words you need to give clues for, and 3 black Assassin words!

  • A Clue must be exactly one word and one number (the number of words it relates to).
  • If you guess a Green Agent correctly, you can keep guessing or pass.
  • If you guess a Grey Bystander, your turn immediately ends, and it costs a turn token.
  • If you hit a Black Assassin, the game is instantly over and you both lose!

Work together, don't look at the other person's Intel, and find all 15 agents to win!
